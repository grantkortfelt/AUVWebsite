About the Electrical Team

The Electrical team focuses on designing and implementing the vehicle's power and control systems. We work on integrating sensors, actuators, and ensuring the vehicle's electrical components are robust and reliable.

Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of underwater technology and ensuring our AUV can operate efficiently in various environments.

Key Responsibilities

The Electrical team is responsible for the following tasks:
* Designing and implementing the vehicle's power systems.
* Integrating sensors and actuators for navigation and task execution.
* Developing and testing circuit boards and electrical components.
* Ensuring the waterproofing and durability of electrical systems.

Team Goals

The Electrical Team aims to innovate and improve the AUV's performance by leveraging cutting-edge technology and ensuring robust electrical systems.
