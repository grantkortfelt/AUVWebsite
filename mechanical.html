About the Mechanical Team

The Mechanical team focuses on the design and construction of the AUV's physical structure. We work on the hull design, propulsion systems, and ensuring the vehicle can withstand underwater conditions.

Our team is dedicated to creating a robust and efficient vehicle that can perform various tasks in underwater environments.

Key Responsibilities

The Mechanical team is responsible for the following tasks:
* Designing the AUV's hull and frame
* Integrating propulsion systems
* Ensuring structural integrity and durability
* Collaborating with other teams to ensure seamless integration of components
* Conducting tests and evaluations of mechanical systems

Team Goals

Our primary goals include improving the AUV's efficiency, enhancing its maneuverability, and ensuring reliability in challenging underwater environments. We aim to innovate and push the boundaries of underwater robotics.
